### 14. Fraud & Cyber-Fraud Security

Banks must include this domain.

- Online banking fraud patterns: Detection and prevention of digital fraud
- Transaction monitoring: Real-time analysis of financial transactions
- Anti-fraud analytics: Machine learning and statistical models for fraud detection
- Payment fraud: Protection against unauthorized payment activities
- Account takeover prevention: Detection and prevention of account compromises
- Device fingerprinting: Identification and tracking of devices
- Behavioral analytics: User behavior analysis for anomaly detection
- Collaboration with fraud teams: Coordination with business fraud units

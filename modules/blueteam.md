### 8. Blue Team / Detection & Response

Operational defense layer.

- SOC operations: 24/7 monitoring, alert triage, incident escalation
- SIEM management: Log aggregation, correlation rules, and platform maintenance
- Detection engineering: Creation and maintenance of detection rules and analytics
- Threat hunting: Proactive searches across telemetry for anomalous behavior
- Incident response and forensics: Playbooks, forensic analysis, and containment
- Malware analysis: Reverse engineering and threat characterization
- Alert tuning and false positive reduction: Improving signal-to-noise ratio
- Case management and reporting: Incident tracking and stakeholder communication
- Logging strategy & telemetry mapping: Data collection and retention policies
- Use case lifecycle (design, test, review): Detection rule development process

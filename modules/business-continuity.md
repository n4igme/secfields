### 15. Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery

Ensuring the bank keeps running.

- BCP & DR strategy: Business continuity and disaster recovery planning
- RTO/RPO definition: Recovery time and point objectives
- Crisis management: Emergency response and communication procedures
- Backup strategy and verification: Data backup and restoration validation
- Tabletop exercises & simulations: Regular testing of continuity plans
- Failover testing: Verification of system failover capabilities
- Pandemic and physical disruptions: Planning for various disruption scenarios
- Communication plans: Internal and external communication during incidents

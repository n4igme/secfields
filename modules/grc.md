### 1. Governance & Strategy

High-level direction, ownership, and structure.

- Security strategy and roadmap: Long-term vision and tactical plans
- Organizational structure: CISO, deputies, domain owners responsibilities
- Security budgets and investment planning: Resource allocation and justification
- Policies, standards, procedures: Framework for security governance
- Security performance metrics and reporting: KPIs and executive dashboards
- Leadership communication: Board reporting and strategic alignment
- Training & awareness strategy: Cultural change and compliance programs
- Third-party and regulatory alignment: Vendor management and oversight

#### 2. Risk Management

How the bank identifies and handles risk.

- Enterprise risk management: Strategic risk identification and treatment
- Cyber risk quantification: Financial impact modeling and risk scoring
- Risk register and scoring methodology: Standardized assessment processes
- Control frameworks: ISO 27001, NIST CSF, PCI DSS implementation
- Operational risk alignment: Integration with business risk frameworks
- Vendor and supply chain risk: Third-party security assessments
- Business impact analysis (BIA): Critical asset and process identification
- Scenario-based risk modeling: Threat modeling and risk scenarios

#### 3. Compliance, Audit & Regulatory

Because banks have heavy obligations.

- Compliance program management: Ongoing regulatory adherence
- Internal audits: Periodic control validation and assessments
- External and regulatory audits: Third-party audit coordination
- Regulatory obligations:
  - Local financial authority rules (e.g., Fed, OCC, FinCEN)
  - PCI DSS for payment card security
  - Data privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, state regulations)
- Control evidence and documentation: Audit trail maintenance
- Continuous compliance monitoring: Automated compliance validation
- Reporting to regulators and board: Regulatory submission and board updates

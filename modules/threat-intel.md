### 9. Threat Intelligence

Understanding actual threats to a bank.

- Threat modeling: STRIDE, attack paths, misuse cases analysis
- Intelligence cycle: Collection, analysis, and distribution processes
- Tracking threat actors: FIN groups, local threat landscape monitoring
- Fraud patterns: Detection of financial crime indicators
- Phishing campaigns: Monitoring and mitigation strategies
- Brand abuse monitoring: Detection of unauthorized use of bank identity
- Dark web monitoring: Identification of stolen credentials and data
- TLP communication practices: Traffic Light Protocol for information sharing

### 12. Data Security

Critical in banking operations.

- Data classification: Identification and labeling of sensitive information
- Data governance: Management of data throughout its lifecycle
- Encryption at rest & in transit: Protection of data in all states
- Tokenization & masking: Protection of sensitive data in non-production environments
- DLP: Data Loss Prevention systems and policies
- Database auditing: Monitoring and logging of database access
- Sensitive data lifecycle: Management from creation to destruction
- Key management: Cryptographic key lifecycle and storage
- Handling of customer data: Regulations and protection requirements

### 2. Risk Management

How the bank identifies and handles risk.

- Enterprise risk management: Strategic risk identification and treatment
- Cyber risk quantification: Financial impact modeling and risk scoring
- Risk register and scoring methodology: Standardized assessment processes
- Control frameworks: ISO 27001, NIST CSF, PCI DSS implementation
- Operational risk alignment: Integration with business risk frameworks
- Vendor and supply chain risk: Third-party security assessments
- Business impact analysis (BIA): Critical asset and process identification
- Scenario-based risk modeling: Threat modeling and risk scenarios

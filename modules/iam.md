### 6. Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Critical for any financial institution.

- Identity lifecycle management: Creation, maintenance, and deprovisioning processes
- Role-based access control (RBAC): Permission models and role definitions
- Privileged access management (PAM): Administrative access controls and monitoring
- MFA strategy: Multi-factor authentication implementation across systems
- Access recertification: Periodic review and validation of user permissions
- Zero Trust identity model: Continuous verification and least privilege access
- Customer identity considerations (CIAM): Consumer authentication and authorization

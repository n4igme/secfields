### 18. Metrics, Reporting & Program Management

How the bank measures and drives security.

- KPIs and KRIs: Key Performance Indicators and Key Risk Indicators
- Risk dashboards: Visualization of security and risk metrics
- Incident metrics: Tracking and analysis of security incidents
- Vulnerability metrics: Measurement of vulnerability management effectiveness
- Exec-level reporting: Board and executive-level security reporting
- Security program maturity assessments: Evaluation of security program effectiveness
- OKRs for cybersecurity: Objectives and Key Results for security teams

### 7. Red Team / Offensive Security

Offensive efforts, but written from organizational perspective.

- Penetration testing program: Regular assessments of network, app, and physical security
- Adversary simulation & threat emulation: Realistic attack scenarios against critical assets
- Purple teaming: Collaborative exercises between offensive and defensive teams
- Vulnerability management lifecycle: Coordinated disclosure and remediation process
- Bughunting & external reporting: Public and private bug bounty programs
- AppSec assessments: Application security testing as part of SDLC
- Social engineering testing: Phishing, vishing, and physical access tests
- Physical security testing: Assessment of physical access controls and procedures
